## Hand-write your answers

You **must** hand write your answers to the questions in the worksheet.  

- One easy way to do this is to print out the worksheet and the write your answers on the print-out.

- Another option is to use a blank piece of paper (with or without horizontal lines - that's up to you). 
  - If you use this option then **make sure that you clearly label which question you're answering!**  Otherwise the instructor won't know which question you're trying to answer.


## Submission

A single PDF file containing images of all your hand-written work.

For work done on paper you can take pictures with your phone, put the images into a single document (Word Doc or Google Doc or Apple Pages or whatever), then save that document into PDF format (there may be an "Export To" menu option that does this, or the "Save As" menu option may do this), then upload that single PDF file.
