## Answers

1. You may take a makeup exam at any time with no penalty
2. You must notify the instructor before the exam starts, then arrange a makeup exam time that both you and the instructor agree on within 48 hours.  The exam itself must be made up within 7 days
3. Missed exams are automatically excused
4. You can submit a video explanation instead
